What is Frontend Web Development?

The term “frontend web development” refers to making the visible and interactive parts of a website or online application. It connects the design and the programming and gives websites their lifelike qualities.

It’s the visible and interactive part, but it rests on a foundation of systems that work in concert to make what you experience possible.

Keep in mind that Frontend Development is only one part of the larger web development process. It is complementary to Backend Development, which handles the application’s “back end” (the server) functionality and data storage and retrieval. As a whole, they constitute the final step in creating a website.

Let’s speak about the many frontend technologies and frameworks that are in high demand right now. There are always new technologies and frameworks appearing in this industry, but right now Angular, React, and Vue.js are three of the most widely used. By providing a set of pre-built components and tools, these frameworks aim to speed up and improve the quality of the development process.

In this regard, Angular stands out as an especially potent and reliable framework for building highly interactive and complicated online apps. It’s popular in the field and an excellent option for individuals looking to focus on the front end of the web development process.
